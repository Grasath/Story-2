Chapter 2: The Dark Secret

As the months passed, Lily continued to visit the enchanted forest. 
She made friends with the animals and learned many secrets about the magical world. 
However, one day, she overheard a conversation between the old lady and a mysterious figure. 
They were discussing a dark secret about the forest, and Lily felt that something was not right. 
She decided to investigate and found out that an evil sorcerer was planning to destroy the forest and build a factory in its place. 
Lily knew that she had to act fast to save the forest and its inhabitants.
